Overview

The COVID-19 is a pandemic disease which has been spread all over the world and millions of people got infected by it.The World Health Organization (WHO) has declared the corona virus disease 2019 (COVID-19) a pandemic. A global coordinated effort is needed to stop the further spread of the virus.A pandemic is defined as “occurring over a wide geographic area and affecting an exceptionally high proportion of the population.”

The project tries to guide the people who are looking for real time information about the pandemic.It monitors data regarding COVID-19 affected over the globe. The data contains how many people got infected, cured and deceased at country level.For better understanding andvisualization we plots the line graph for the same data fetched.
